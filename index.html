Road Safety Rule

Road Safety Rules And Regulations:-

1)Keep To Your Left – Always drive or ride on the left side of the road and let other vehicles overtake you from the right side.

2)Turn Right – Come to the centre of the road before you start turning right but when going around the curb, try to stay towards the left-most part of your lane to avoid contact with oncoming traffic.

3)Always Overtake from the right side. 4)When being overtaken by another vehicle, never increase your speed to prevent the other driver from overtaking you.

5)Be extra careful on intersections. Also, when passing through them, ensure your vehicle doesn’t cause inconvenience to other road users.

6)Turn Right- Always give a right of way to vehicles on intersections by letting them continue without stopping in that particular direction in which you are about to proceed.

7)Emergency Vehicles – It is your responsibility to give way to emergency services vehicles such as fire engines and ambulances.

8)Pedestrians have the right of way at pedestrian crossings or zebra crossings.

9)“U” Turns- U-turns can only be taken when there is no warning sign nearby you give a proper indication to other vehicle drivers that you are going to take a U turn
